Visualization of Taipei Housing Information

Final Demo | Name: 林嘉欣, 邵文強, 林劍湃, 劉榮 | Sid: 108AEA002,108AEA003,108AEA005,108AEA007

For traditional Chinese, buying a house is one of the necessary requirements for living in peace and setting up a business. For fresh graduates, they do not understand the price factors of the real estate market, so spend their whole lives to pay off some unnecessary intermediate price differences. Therefore, we will collect historical relevant housing prices, locations and related influencing factors in the past ten years, analyze the relationship between location factors and real estate prices, analyze housing price trends and actual prices, and provide buyers with decision-making references.

套房

指為了在同一房間內滿足多個起居需要，把多個設施在嵌套房間裡，而形成房中房的房間結構。

透天厝

即房子座落的土地的產權跟建物一樣都能完全持有，不須跟別人持分。

公寓

為4-5層樓沒有電梯的集合住宅，屋齡約為20-40年。

華廈

10層以下樓並擁有電梯的集合住宅。

大樓

超過10層樓並擁有電梯的集合住宅，大部份配有中庭花園、地下停車場、警衛管理室、健身房、游泳池等多樣公共設施。
